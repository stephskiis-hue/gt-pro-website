Our Story

From a workshop vision to
Winnipeg's Finest.

GT PRO ENTERPRISES was founded in 2025 with a single premise: that luxury renovation in Winnipeg shouldn't be about expensive materials alone — it should be about the luxury of thoughtful, precise execution.

Our founders spent their early years restoring older Winnipeg character homes across Wolseley, St. Boniface, and the Exchange District, developing an eye for the "invisible details" — the way light hits a tile joint, the tactile weight of a solid brass pull, and the structural honesty of stone.

Today, our team has grown across kitchens, bathrooms, and basements throughout Manitoba — but our core ethos remains unchanged. We don't just renovate; we curate the environments where your life's finest moments unfold.
